About Me

I'm a product manager and technologist living in Brooklyn. I manage the software that powers the holographic displays made at Looking Glass. I grew up in Vancouver, Canada, did my MFA at the NYU Game Center, and enjoy writing and making weird digital experiences. I like to hike, kayak, bird watch, play chess, and read speculative fiction.

My creative and critical work often centers on the way humans relate to non-human animals and the natural environment, the way natural spaces are depicted in digital media, and how novel technologies impact social structures.
