About Me

I'm a game developer and digital media artist living in Brooklyn. For my day job, I make holograms at Looking Glass. I grew up in Vancouver, Canada, did my MFA at the NYU Game Center, and enjoy writing and making weird digital experiences. I like to hike, kayak, bird watch, play chess, and read speculative fiction.

My work often centers on the way humans relate to non-human animals and the natural environment, and the way natural spaces are depicted in digital media.
