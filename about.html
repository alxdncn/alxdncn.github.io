About Me

I am an independent game developer living in New York City. I spent the last two years analysing and learning how to make games as part of my MFA at the New York University Game Center. Before then, I wrote about games and studied English literature and economics at the University of British Columbia.

My work often centers on the way us humans relate to non-human animals and the natural environment. In work related to this, as well as other topics, I draw from my background in literature as well as my interest in critical theory.

For inquiries about game development and writing freelance work, or for any other inquiries, please get in touch.
